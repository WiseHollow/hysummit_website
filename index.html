Claims Custom Plugin

Creating a Claim is likely one of the first things you'll want to do in the survival world. Here is a brief summary of how some feature of claims work.

General Explanation

  1. Create a new Claim with the command /CLAIM DEFINE.
  2. Opening the Claim Editor can be done with the command /CLAIM.
  3. Claims cost 8 money per horizontal block being claimed. The price will always be subject to change.
  4. Redefining a claims boundaries will debit/credit your wallet depending on if the claim is larger or smaller than before.
  5. Player and Guest permissions are set by the owner of the claim. An explanation of flags will be explained below.

Claim Flags

Flags are useful to restrict certain abilities within the boundaries of your claim. All but the first three flags cost money. Buying them will enable them permanently within that claim. Abandoning your claim will not result in a credit of those enabled flags.

A list of currently supported flags are-

  1. Build
  2. Chest Access
  3. Interact
  4. Chorus Fruit Teleport
  5. Ender Pearl Teleport
  6. Damage Animals
  7. Pickup Items
  8. Throw Items
  9. Explosives
  10. Monster Spawning
  11. Animal Spawning

Equipment Extensions Custom Plugin

Plain vanilla equipment stats are boring. That's why I created a plugin that adds a bit more wonder to items in the game. All weapons and armor in the game will have modified attributes from the "Variant" or "Title" given to the item's name. For example: Great! has a set of attributes that modify a weapon or armor. It'll automatically take effect depending on which it's bound to.

Variant Ranks

The list of available ranks are: F → D → C → B → A → S → SS → SSS

With F being a horrible rank that will result in you being overall weaker than without the item, and C being around vanilla stats. Everything B and after result in stronger overall stats.

Below are ranks with their distribution weight, mob's chance to drop an item, and chance for Variant to appear in the world.

  1. SSS Weight 1 & Item Drop Chance 10% 0.08%
  2. SS Weight 5 & Item Drop Chance 10% 0.4%
  3. S Weight 15 & Item Drop Chance 10% 1.2%
  4. A Weight 100 & Item Drop Chance 8.5% 8%
  5. B Weight 200 & Item Drop Chance 7.5% 16%
  6. C Weight 400 & Item Drop Chance 5% 32%
  7. D Weight 300 & Item Drop Chance 3.5% 24%
  8. F Weight 200 & Item Drop Chance 2.5% 16%

Biome Portals Custom Plugin

What if you could get an alert in advanced of a portal that will teleport you to the biome you're looking for? That's what you'll find with our Biome Portals. Every hour of the day, the portal at spawn will update to a new location and biome. A broadcast in chat and Discord will alert players of it's new destination.

Example broadcast

Biome Portal destination is now MOOSHROOM ISLAND

The portal at spawn will transform into a design that reflects the destination. There is no guessing what you'll meet on the other side.

  1. Plains
  2. Birch Forest
  3. Oak Forest
  4. Savanna
  5. Ice Spikes
  6. Mushroom Fields
  7. Flower Forest
  8. Deep Cold Ocean
  9. And many more..

Biome Chooser

A Villager NPC stands in front of the portal awaiting players to trigger addition random destinations. Interacting with the NPC, you'll be presented with a shop interface. At the cost of ingame cash, you can roll the dice and get a new destination. Be aware, there is a cooldown for this action.

Pets

Do you want a loyal buddy following you around? With our server Pets you can have exactly that. Not only can you Name them, but you can ride and do all sorts of things. Some Pets give you the freedom to even fly around.

Commands

  1. /pet - Opens the Pet GUI.
  2. /pet help - List details on pet commands
  3. /pet ride - Toggle riding your pet
  4. /pet hat - Toggle wearing your pet on your head
  5. /pet list - Lists all the pets allowed on the server

Pet Customizations

Once you right-click on your pet, you'll be presented with the Pet Data Changer. This lets you apply visual customizations to your pet. The options are different among each pet, which is all the more reason to rank up and get access to even more pets.

  1. Name your pet (with chat colors)
  2. Baby/Adult
  3. Appear on fire or not
  4. Appear frozen or not (visibly shaking)
  5. Some pets have color choices
  6. Ability to ride the pet
  7. Ability to wear the pet as a hat
  8. And many more..

Getting Money

There are a few ways to get ingame money while playing. Selling items at spawn, or creating your own shop are two methods. Another method is to defeat mobs around the survival world.

To sell items to NPCs, you must find the correct shop to sell to. Not all items in the game can be purchased and sold, but most that require a degree of grinding are sellable. For example, you can sell carrots and other crops at the grocery store.

To create your own player shop; either head to the dock at spawn-town to rent a space, or create one in the survival world. There are directions on how to do this at the docks.

Lastly, defeated mobs drop a sunflower item. Picking it up will reward you with a range of ingame money. Please note that mobs from a spawner will not drop money.
